Constellation logics

→

Further thinking about patterns. This is perhapse a thinking in reverse from exploring how singular selections of treadling and setup create patterns, forms and shapes. The constallation as one of many mappings of points, of connections drawn and redrawn. The constallation as one of many possible containers, one of many possible reductions, framings and (re)arrangements. Ways of exposing what is bound up, entwined and interwoven.
